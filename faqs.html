Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are your opening hours?

We are open daily from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM, including weekends and public holidays.

2. Do you offer food delivery services?

Yes, we deliver within a 10km radius. You can place orders through our website or food delivery platforms.

3. Is there a student discount?

Yes, students get 10% off with a valid student ID. Look out for our weekly student specials too!

4. How can I provide feedback?

You can use the Feedback form on our website to share your experience.

5. Do you cater for events or group orders?

Absolutely! Visit the Group page to learn more about our group packages and services.
